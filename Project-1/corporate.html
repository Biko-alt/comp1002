Sony Corporation, founded in 1946, is a global leader in electronics, entertainment, and gaming. With a commitment to innovation and quality, Sony has consistently delivered cutting-edge products that enhance the lives of consumers worldwide.

The Sony TR-63 is a testament to our dedication to pioneering technology. As the world's first pocket-sized transistor radio, it set new standards for portability and convenience in audio devices.
